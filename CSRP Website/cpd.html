DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

RANK STRUCTURE

Climb the chain of command.

Members progress through the department based on activity, professionalism, training and leadership performance.

01

COMMAND

Chief of Police

Leads the department and oversees all operations, divisions and department leadership.

02

COMMAND

Assistant Chief

Supports the Chief and assists with major department decisions, standards and operations.

03

COMMAND

Deputy Chief

Oversees command staff, department activity and operational performance.

04

SENIOR LEADERSHIP

Commander

Supervises divisions and coordinates department-wide operations and training.

05

SENIOR LEADERSHIP

Captain

Leads teams, supports supervisors and manages operational standards during sessions.

06

SUPERVISORY

Lieutenant

Supervises officers and assists with training, patrol leadership and discipline.

07

SUPERVISORY

Sergeant

Provides direct supervision during sessions and supports officers in the field.

08

FIELD OPERATIONS

Corporal

A trusted officer who supports supervisors and helps guide newer department members.

09

FIELD OPERATIONS

Senior Officer

An experienced patrol officer with strong department knowledge and consistent performance.

10

FIELD OPERATIONS

Police Officer

Handles patrol duties, traffic enforcement, emergency response and community safety.

11

ENTRY LEVEL

Probationary Officer

A newly trained officer completing supervised patrol and department evaluation.

12

ENTRY LEVEL

Cadet

An applicant currently completing training, evaluation and department onboarding.
